iv) Fragments in thymeleaf (HOW TO CREATE FRAGMENTS)

    <div th:fragment="parent(content)">
        <h1>Parent Fragment</h1>
        <p>Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet consectetur adipisicing elit. Id, ipsam!</p>
        <div th:replace="${content}"></div>
    </div>  

v) How to insert/replace Fragment
    <!-- Using Fragment -->
    <div th:replace="~{base::parent(~{::section})}">
        <section>
            <h1>This is Dynamic Services</h1>
            <p>This is the content of the services page.</p>
            <button>Our Services</button>
        </section>  
    </div>

vi) How to  use expression

    <div th:replace="${isLogin} ? ~{base::parent(~{::section})} : ~{base::test(~{::#not_logged_in})}"></div>

        <section>
            <h1>This is Dynamic About Us</h1>
            <h1>About Us</h1>
